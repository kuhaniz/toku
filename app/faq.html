Getting Started

1. How do I install or download TOKU?

TOKU is a mobile phone app available through the App Store (Apple) or Play Store (Android) on your mobile phone. Click the links above to download the app through your mobile phone.

2. How do I register for an account?

Easy peasy. Download the app following Q1 and follow three simple steps to get going.

Step 1: Enter your country code and mobile phone number into your TOKU app
Step 2: Receive an SMS on your mobile phone containing a 4-digit OTP (One Time Password)
Step 3: Enter the OTP you received into the verification screen on your TOKU app

Voila! You’re done and ready to make your first call.

3. I am having trouble with my OTP (One Time Password)

Your OTP is unique and only valid for 60 seconds, so be quick!

Requesting for a new OTP or entering the wrong OTP too many times may result in your account being locked to protect your security for up to 24 hours. If your account is locked, contact us.

4. Do I need a callname? How do I choose one?

The answer is yes! Every TOKU user needs a callname to make and receive calls.

Your callname has to be a minimum of 3, to a maximum of 30 characters in length and should only consist of alphabets, numbers and/or the underscore _ symbol.

Your callname is permanent and will be shared with people you want to receive web calls from... so choose carefully!

5. What if the TOKU callname I want is taken?

If your chosen callname is taken, you will have to choose another one, as all callnames are on a first-come-first-served basis.

If you are a corporate or business user and your company callname is taken, contact us.

6. What is my personal TOKU web call link? How do I use it?

Your personal webcall link is attached to your callname. The URL to follow is:

http://my.tokuapp.com/yourcallnamehere

Note: Anyone who accesses your TOKU webcall link can make a call to you right away – no registration or lengthy authentication processes needed. So get sharing!

7. Do I need to share my e-mail with TOKU?

Yes. Your e-mail address is required from time to time to ensure that your account details can be backed up, should you ever change or lose your phone number, so don’t take too long to verify your e-mail address.

We do send the occasional e-mail containing important account updates, exclusive content and special promotions that you can unsubscribe from at any time. Your data is safe and will never be shared with any third parties. That’s our TOKU promise!

Making calls

8. How do I make a call with TOKU?

Easy. Open the app and navigate to the Contacts section. Click on the Phone Icon next to the name of the person you’d like to call... and TOKU will get you connected.

TOKU Recommends: Best Available Call Option
Depending on where you are, or who you are calling, our TOKU-tastic technology will provide you with the best available call option, in realtime.

Prefer a different call option? Not a problem.

Just click on the horizontal ellipsis icon (it looks like three dots … ) located at the bottom right of the screen. From there, you will be able to choose from the other call options available.

9. What are all the call options TOKU has?

We have five TOKU-mazing call options at present:

OPTION 1: FREE CALL
How it works: Make a call over your internet or data connection.

It’s free for a TOKU user (you) call another TOKU user (friend) over a stable internet connection.

To find out if your friend is already a TOKU user, click on the “Contacts” section, followed by the “TOKU” tab. All contacts displayed there are fellow TOKU users that you can call for free. Just click on their name and call away!

Note: Free Call requires an Internet connection or Mobile Data. Any charges incurred for these services with your local Internet and/or Mobile Service Provider (MSP) will apply.

OPTION 2: CALL OUT
How it works: Make a call over your internet or data connection to a mobile or landline number anywhere in the world - for one of the lowest call rates around.

Call out lets you make calls to any number (both mobile and landline!) in any country worldwide for a very affordable rate.

Note: Call Out requires an Internet connection or Mobile Data. Any charges incurred for these services with your local Internet and/or Mobile Service Provider (MSP) will apply.

OPTION 3: CALL THRU (*Available in Singapore only)
How it works: Make a local call from your mobile or landline to a local TOKU number and get connected to any International Number of your choice.

Call Thru allows you to make an International call to any mobile or landline without the expensive international call charges. Just make a regular local call (mobile or landline) to the TOKU local number provided, and we take care of the rest!

Note: Call Thru is only available in Singapore at the moment, but we are wokring hard to roll this feature out to more countries. In SG? Your local outgoing voice call charges will apply.

OPTION 4: CALL BACK
How it works: Make a request to call a contact and TOKU calls you back once connected.

If you choose the Call Back option next to a contact on the TOKU app, our clever servers will call you back and connect you to the number attached to the contact requested.

No Internet connection for your app to work? Not a problem, TOKU can also send Call Back requests to the TOKU servers via SMS.

Note: Call Back requires incoming voice calls and your MSP voice call charges will apply.

OPTION 5: NATIVE CALL
How it works: This option exits TOKU and makes a standard call from your mobile.

On the rare occasion that we have to go back to basics, TOKU will connect your call outside of the app through the standard dialer pad on your mobile.

Note: Native call requires outgoing calls and your MSP voice call charges will apply.

10. Can I try TOKU before deciding to purchase credit?

Sure you can. And your first call is on us!

When you sign up, you will automatically receive USD $0.20 to call the world. This will get you about 3 to 15 minutes’ worth of talk time, depending on the country you’re calling.

Try us out. We can’t wait to impress you with our amazing call quality!

11. Can I use TOKU when I am overseas?

Yes you can! However, the relevant Internet or Data charges will apply, please check the source of your internet connection or data roaming plan through your MSP (Mobile Service Provider) prior to use.

If you are a Singapore user, Call Thru will incur roaming charges as set by your MSP.

12. How do I view my Call Logs?

Head to the “Recent” section and click on the contact to view your call log by contact.

13. Can I dial emergency numbers with TOKU?

Sorry, we don’t support emergency calls. Please make a regular call for these services.

Topping up

14. How do I top up my TOKU?

Click on the “More” section in the app and click on the “Top Up” tab. We offer three credit options for top up – USD $9.90, $14.90 and $19.90 for you to choose from.

APPLE IOS: You can top up via in-app purchases.
ANDROID: You can top up via the app, which will redirect to PayPal to complete the transaction.

Callforwarding

15. How do I enable the Call Forwarding function on TOKU?

Click the “More” section and click on the Call Forwarding tab. On there you can select the number(s) you want to activate call forwarding to. You can register up to 2 phone numbers too.

Never miss a call again!
Setting up call forwarding means that you will always receive calls made to your TOKU account.

16. How do I add additional numbers to forward my calls to?

In the Call Forwarding section, click the “+” sign at the top right-hand corner of your screen.

Select the country code of the number you want to add (you will see it in the list of available regions).. Then enter the number you wish to forward to, and an SMS containing a 4-digit OTP (One Time Password) will be sent to your mobile for verification..Key in the OTP into the TOKU verification screen and you are ready to go.., easy as pie!

Note: To add a number successfully, you have to verify it first.

Vouchers

17. I have received a TOKU voucher code? How do I redeem it?

Navigate to the “More” section in the app and click on the “Redeem Voucher” tab. Once you key in the voucher code, you will receive the credits.

If your voucher vode requires you to top up in order to redeem the credits, you will be redirected to the Top Up page to continue to process.

18. My TOKU voucher code requires me to top up before redeeming it. How do I do this?

Click on the “More” section followed by the “Top Up” tab. Select the value of credits you wish to purchase (in USD $9.90, $14.90 or USD $19.90 denominations).

Enter your TOKU voucher code at checkout and proceed to complete the transaction.

19. My TOKU voucher code doesn’t work. Help!

This could be because you may have already used the specific voucher code already, or it may have expired.

If you feel this is not the case, contact us and we will look into what went wrong.
